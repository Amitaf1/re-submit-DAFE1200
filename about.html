About us

History

In 2020, a total of over 80 percent of consumers across the globe shopped online. This isn't surprising, considering retail e-commerce sales worldwide has been on an exponential rise since 2004.

What we do

Donating is easy enough, but sometimes we forget. Here at Shop4Good, we're here to help you, so that you wouldn't need to always remember. By downloading your extension on your browser, we'll help you donate a small percentage of money to the charities of choosing every time you shop online.

Instructions

First set up an account here on our website, and add how many charity organizations you want. Then download our extention and you're work is done! You can of course choose to stop whenever you see fit.

What's in it for the companies?

As a company, donating will only be beneficial for you. From tax-benefits, to deductions, this is your chance to join us!
